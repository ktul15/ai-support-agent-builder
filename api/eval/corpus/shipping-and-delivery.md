# Shipping and Delivery

We roast to order and hand your coffee to the carrier within 2 business days of
roasting.

## Where we ship
Brightbrew ships to the United States and Canada only. We do not ship anywhere
else at this time.

## Delivery times and cost
- United States: 3–5 business days. Shipping is a flat $5 per order (free on the
  Pro plan).
- Canada: 5–8 business days. Shipping to Canada is a flat $9 per order.

## Tracking
When your order ships, we email you a tracking number so you can follow it to
your door.
