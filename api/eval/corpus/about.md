# About Brightbrew

Brightbrew is a specialty coffee subscription service founded in 2021 and based
in Portland, Oregon. We roast every order to order and ship it within 2 business
days of roasting, so your coffee arrives as fresh as possible.

We source our green beans from three origins: Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.
Each origin is available in three roast levels — light, medium, and dark — so you
can dial in exactly the cup you like.

Brightbrew is an online subscription service only. We do not operate a physical
cafe or retail store; all coffee is delivered to your door.
