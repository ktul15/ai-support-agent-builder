# Returns and Refunds

## 30-day satisfaction guarantee
If you are not happy with your coffee for any reason, contact us within 30 days
of delivery for a full refund.

Because coffee is perishable, you do not need to return the bag to get a refund —
keep it or share it with a friend.

## How refunds are processed
Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method within 5 business
days.

## Cancelling or pausing
You can cancel or pause your subscription at any time from your account settings.
There is no cancellation fee.
