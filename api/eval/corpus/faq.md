# Frequently Asked Questions

## Grind
Coffee ships as whole bean by default. At checkout you can choose to have it
ground for drip, espresso, or French press instead.

## Decaf
Decaf is available on every plan. Our decaf is a Colombian bean.

## Storage and freshness
Store your coffee in an airtight container in a cool, dark place, and use it
within 4 weeks of the roast date for the best flavor.

## Managing your account
You can change your plan at any time from your dashboard, where your next order
date is also shown.

## Gift subscriptions
Brightbrew offers gift subscriptions in 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month options.
