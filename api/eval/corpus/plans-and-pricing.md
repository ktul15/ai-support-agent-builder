# Plans and Pricing

Brightbrew offers three monthly subscription plans. All prices are in US dollars.

## Taster — $12/month
- One 6 oz bag per month.
- A good way to try a single origin or roast.

## Standard — $24/month
- Two 12 oz bags per month.
- Mix and match origins and roast levels.

## Pro — $45/month
- Four 12 oz bags per month.
- Includes free shipping within the US.

## Billing and flexibility
- Every plan can be paused or cancelled at any time — there is no cancellation
  fee and no long-term contract.
- Choose annual billing to save 15% compared with paying monthly.
- Shipping is a flat $5 per order on the Taster and Standard plans. Shipping is
  free on the Pro plan.
